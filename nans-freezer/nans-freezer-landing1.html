Our Story

What started as a simple idea turned into something magical. When their dad discovered an old food trailer at a great price, a brother and sister duo saw an opportunity to create something special together.

With determination, creativity, and lots of hard work, these young entrepreneurs transformed that worn trailer into a vibrant ice cream shop on wheels. They learned about starting a business, mastered proper food handling, studied for inspections, and discovered what it takes to turn a dream into reality.

But this isn't just any ice cream business. Every scoop served, every smile shared, and every moment of joy created is dedicated to someone very special who inspired it all.
